Project: The Future of ResolveISH

ResolveISH was born out of the need to create a seamless, transparent cross-bank resolution tool that puts power back in the hands of everyday customers. In Nigeria, customers often face unexplained bank deductions, failed reversals, and missing funds—with no clear, reliable path to resolution. ResolveISH is the centralized platform that empowers users to report, track, and resolve bank-related complaints—quickly and transparently.
